# Information Technology Act, 2000

This folder contains the IT Act and its various amendments, rules, and related regulations.

## Current Status

### Information Technology Act, 2000 (as amended)
- **Original Enactment**: October 17, 2000
- **Major Amendment**: IT (Amendment) Act, 2008 (October 27, 2008)
- **Status**: Operative

## Key Rules Under IT Act

### IT (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021
- **Notification Date**: February 25, 2021
- **Amendments**: April 2023, others
- **Key Provisions**:
  - Due diligence by intermediaries
  - Significant Social Media Intermediaries (SSMI) obligations
  - Grievance redressal mechanism
  - Content moderation requirements
  - Digital news publishers code

### Other Relevant Rules
- IT (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011
- IT (The Indian CERT) Rules, 2013
- IT (Procedure and Safeguards for Blocking) Rules, 2009
- IT (Procedure and Safeguards for Monitoring and Collection) Rules, 2009

## Key Provisions

- **Section 66A**: Struck down by Supreme Court (Shreya Singhal)
- **Section 69**: Power to intercept/monitor
- **Section 69A**: Power to block websites
- **Section 79**: Intermediary liability safe harbor
- **Section 43A**: Data security and compensation

## Proposed Replacement

- **Digital India Act**: Proposed to replace IT Act 2000
- Status: Consultation phase

## Landmark Cases

See `sources/judgements/` for cases tagged with "IT-Act":
- Shreya Singhal v. Union of India (2015) - Section 66A unconstitutional
- Various Section 69A/blocking cases
